## Overview
**Mundo IEL** is a virtual reality and mobile application created to simulate educational environments for classes and lectures. It blends 3D modeling with dynamic asset delivery to address the constraints of mobile distribution.

## ‚ú® Solution

### Modeling Pipeline
- Created environments using Blender and Gravity Sketch.
- Focused on modularity for efficient reuse of assets across scenes.

- Enabled remote team interactions within the modeling process.
